We're

Who are we in the next 15 years?

With the appearance of different individuals with unique personalities, but the same passion for coding, Sandbox Technology is a team of 5 members where we explore and get creative with coding, which is what we are all passionate about. Sandbox is like a shallow box in the ground party filled with sand for children to play in, have fun without pain because it wouldn’t hurt if you tripped or fell into the sand pit, which fits with our goals. But for Sandbox Technology, it doesn't just mean a shallow box for us to play on. It also means an isolated testing environment that allows users to run programs without affecting the application or platform, making it relevant to our passion: coding.

Thanks to Codeavengers, Sandbox Technology was able to level up their coding skills into another level, learning different language codes, UI designs, computer science, programming, and more, so we can apply our knowledge and understanding to our upcoming projects to help and benefit people around us.
